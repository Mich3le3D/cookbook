## Ingredienti

* [ ] `························` | Filetti di alici
* [ ] `························` | Pomodori
* [ ] `························` | Olive
* [ ] `························` | Basilico
* [ ] `························` | Olio di oliva
* [ ] `························` | Sale
* [ ] `························` | Pepe

## Preparazione

* Disporre nella teglia, nell'ordine, olio, filetti di alici rigorosamente asciutti, pomodoro tagliato a pezzetti, olive, basilico, olio, sale, pepe.

## Cottura

Cuocere in forno preriscaldato ventilato a `180°C` per `≈10-15min`.
